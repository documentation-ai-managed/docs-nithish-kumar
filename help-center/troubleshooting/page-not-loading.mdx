---
title: Documentation page not loading
description: How to resolve if a documentation page won't load.
---

## Symptoms

- Page stays blank
- "Failed to load" error

## Possible causes

- Browser cache issue
- Internet connection

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````text
    Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your internet">
    Make sure you are online and refresh the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
