## Overview

Organize your docs by creating sections and using tags.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create section">
    In your project, click "Add Section" and name it (e.g. Getting Started).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a page">
    Click "Add Page" in your section to start new documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tag for search">
    Use tags on your page for faster search and filtering.
  </Step>
</Steps>
